RESEARCH

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Discovering our competitors will help us analyze what is already in the market and find ways to further improve the experience. We do this by conducting SWOT and UX analysis to gain deeper understanding to help identify potential innovation through design. We have found that these apps are commonly used to get people organized and accountable with household utilities and responsibilities. Number one being the household chores.

USER INTERVIEW

The goal is to identify the journey our users go through who have been or are in a house share experience. We have conducted 10 user interviews ranging from 22 to 40 years of age.

SYNTHESIS

We then extracted the information and synthesized our findings. Grouping and altering them until we find connections or stories as to what is really happening in a house share.

FINDINGS

• People who live with someone who are in some form of a relationship for example a workmate, a friend or a friend of a friend are more likely to have a bad house share experience than those who live with complete strangers. This is the case because people who tend to live with strangers are more cautious therefore intensive interviews are conducted and house rules are being communicated.

• A house share experience becomes successful when two or more people share the same values.

• People are reluctant to confront housemates to avoid tension or uncomfortable situations. In some cases they avoid confrontation altogether because they think the results might be worse or the fear of being judged.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

“Charlie needs a way to communicate household expectations so that he can avoid feeling anxious and avoid future conflict with housemates”

HOW MIGHT WE'S

• HMW be able to let the housemates be reminded of household utilities so Charlie won’t have to approach them?
• HMW be able to let Charlie and his housemate know what is to be expected living in his place?
• HMW be able to let Charlie express his concerns without making him feel anxious doing so?

HYPOTHESIS

We believe that by creating an app that facilitates and mediates within the household for Charlie, We will create and encourage more harmonious living for both housemates. We know this to be true if both parties are able to meet awareness of house expectations and have minimal confrontation as possible.
